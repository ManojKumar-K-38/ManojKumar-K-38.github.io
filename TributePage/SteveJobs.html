Steve Jobs

The great visionary and philanthropist.

Timeline of Steve Jobs' life:

1950s - 1970s: Early Life and Education

  • 24 Feb 1955 - Steve Jobs is born in San Francisco, California, and adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs.
  • 1968 - Meets Steve Wozniak through a mutual friend.
  • 1972 - Graduates from Homestead High School, Cupertino.
  • 1972 - Enrolls at Reed College in Oregon but drops out after one semester.
  • 1974 - Works at Atari and travels to India in search of spiritual enlightenment.

1976 - 1985: Apple's Beginnings and Rise

  • 1976 - Co-founds Apple Computer Inc. with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne in his parents' garage.
  • 1977 - Apple II is released - one of the first successful mass-market computers.
  • 1980 - Apple goes public; Jobs becomes a multimillionaire.
  • 1983 - Recruits PepsiCo president John Sculley to be Apple CEO.
  • 1984 - Apple introduces the Macintosh - the first mass-market personal computer with a graphical user interface.
  • 1985 - Following internal conflicts, Jobs resigns from Apple after being sidelined.

1985 - 1996: NeXT and Pixar

  • 1985 - Founds NeXT Inc., focusing on high-end computers for higher education and business.
  • 1986 - Buys The Graphics Group (later renamed Pixar) from Lucasfilm for $10 million.
  • 1995 - Pixar's "Toy Story" is released — the first full-length computer-animated film — and becomes a massive success.
  • 1996 - Apple announces it will acquire NeXT for $429 million, bringing Jobs back to Apple.

1997 - 2011: Apple's Revival and Legacy

  • 1997 - Returns to Apple as interim CEO.
  • 1998 - Launches the iMac — beginning of Apple's design renaissance.
  • 2001 - Introduces the iPod, revolutionizing music consumption.
  • 2003 - Diagnosed with a rare form of pancreatic cancer (islet cell neuroendocrine tumor).
  • 2007 - Unveils the iPhone, redefining smartphones.
  • 2010 - Launches the iPad, opening the tablet market.
  • 24 Aug 2011 - Resigns as Apple CEO, becomes chairman.
  • 5 Oct 2011 - Steve Jobs dies at the age of 56.

Posthumous

  • Oct 2011 - Tim Cook becomes CEO of Apple.
  • 2015 - Movie Steve Jobs, directed by Danny Boyle and written by Aaron Sorkin, is released.
  • 2017 - Steve Jobs Theater opens at Apple Park.
  • 2022 - Posthumously awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom.
